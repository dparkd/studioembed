ITEM 3.    PROCESS, CAPABILITIES, AND SCOPE OF WORK

The best way to explain how we work is by comparing it to a tennis rally. Just like in a rally, you want a partner who can challenge you while keeping the game enjoyable. We serve as your rally partners in creativity. A partner who can adapt to where you are in your journey will help reignite inspiration in both you and your product. With that in mind, here's how the process unfolds if you choose to work with us:

•

To kick things off, you serve the ball. You'll onboard us to your product, your vision, and your team, treating us as if we're joining as a founding designer.

•

Next, we'll analyze what you sent. We take a deep dive into the information and figure out how to create a body of work that will either inspire you or address the problem.

•

Then, we hit the ball back. We present our findings and proposed solutions in the form of a prototype or slide deck.

•

Finally, you decide your next move. You can either choose to iterate on what we've developed or end the engagement.

We are only currently available for an hourly rate. No full-time.
