STUDIO EMBED.

A STRATEGY AND DESIGN PARTNER FOR CREATIVITY

See Work Get in touch

I am a partner who can expand possibilities and navigate the ambiguous. Studio Embed's thesis is to answer questions by asking more questions. This is manifested through searchlight exploration and low level creativity. There are three principles that Studio Embed carry: craft from design first principles, explore the unknown, and enable creativity. The reason why I named my practice Studio Embed is because I optimize for high touch collaboration. While I design craft products, I am also embedded in all aspects of the culture and company. I strive to produce an environment of craft and excellence for workflows, collaboration, and product execution.

If you are a founder, company, or builder who cares about building a taste-led product, then you loop me in. Good enough is not enough anymore. You pull me in when you are serious about building experiences that cut through the norm with a high level of craft and taste. I am a design strategist who can trigger excitement, invoke inspiration, and produce creative energy.

TYPES OF ENGAGEMENT

Available q3 2024

This is not an exhaustive list of studio capabilities, but a list to give you a good sense of what type of collaboration would work. Although this is a list of things I could do, I much prefer meeting with you in real time to discuss what type of creative collaboration we can explore. I will tailor my partnership with you.

•

"Product audit + insights deck" refers to me doing a deep dive of your product and generating a slide deck of insights for improvements, things to think about, and first thoughts.

•

"Prompt exploration" refers to me exploring and protoyping a solution for a prompt that you care about.

•

"Light touch sprint" refers to me running a design sprint exploring a space that has not yet been explored by your team.

•

"High touch sprint" refers to me creating a vision for your product and building it alongside your team.

•

"Design mentorship" refers to me mentoring your junior designers in both mechanical and thinking skills.

•

"Critique guest" refers to me joining in a critique and providing insights from the lens of craft.

•

"Origami class" refers to me teaching how to prototype fluid interfaces using Origami.
