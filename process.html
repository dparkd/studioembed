What am I interested in?

Areas of focus

Tools for creativity

Creation, Editing, AI, Conversational

I've always been interested in building and designing tools for creativity. The best tools in the world are one's that speak for themselves and have affordances that don't require too many instructions. All tools should allow for progressive mastery, but on the surface simple enough to enable creativity.



Playful interfaces

Play, delight, interactions, spice

As a craftsperson, I care deeply about building and designing the most beautiful products for other craftspeople. My focus isn't to just solve the problem, but to create delightful software. When you're designing for creatives, the tool needs to be intuitive, look good, and feel good.



Re-imagining Paradigms

Operating Systems, paradigms, blue-sky thinking

Recently, I've had this thesis that all software needs to be redesigned from the ground up. Old affordances and interfaces don't adapt well to the changing landscape of innovation. I am on a mission to redesign and re-imagine all software for the modern day.

Design Process

How I'll cook

Phase 1: Integrate and Immerse

Download and mind meld

Before touching any design tools or pixels, I want to make sure that I'm on the same page. I'm going to be reading all your key documents/presentations, listening to your user interviews, chatting with you, and testing out your product.



Phase 2: Exploration and Synthesis

Initial designs

After getting on the same page as you, I'm going to go explore solo with all the requirements, context, and download that I've done to come up with a thesis and direction.



Phase 3: Ted Talk

Sharing my findings

After cooking, I'll return with a slide deck and a proposal of a potential direction. During this Ted Talk, you'll also input any changes and possible paths you want me to explore the next round.



Phase 4: Execution

Time to cook

With the new feedback and getting on the same page, I'll go ahead and complete the SPEC documents so that they are implementation ready.



Phase 5: Final Delivery

Final presentation + Assets

The final phase in our engagement will be me to present the final designs, assets, and documents.



[Optional] Phase 6: Development support

Helping out while you build

At an hourly rate, I will be available for you after our engagement is finished to help with any questions, extra design work, or discussions.
